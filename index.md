# Privacy Policy for AI Voice Changer & Recorder

**Last Updated: December 11, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to AI Voice Changer & Recorder ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Data
- **Voice Recordings**: The App allows you to record audio using your device's microphone. All recordings are stored **locally on your device only**.
- **Text-to-Speech**: Text you enter for voice generation is processed locally using your device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine.

### Permissions Required
- **Microphone**: Required to record audio for voice transformation features.
- **Storage**: Required to save and access audio files on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

- All audio processing happens **entirely on your device**.
- We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.
- We do **NOT** have access to your recordings or any content you create.
- We do **NOT** use analytics or tracking services.

## Data Storage

- All audio files and app settings are stored locally on your device.
- You have full control over your data and can delete recordings at any time through the App or your device's file manager.
- Uninstalling the App will remove all associated data from your device.

## Third-Party Services

This App does **NOT** use any third-party services that collect data, including:
- No analytics tools
- No advertising networks
- No cloud storage services
- No external APIs

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App processes all data locally and does not transmit any information.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers, your recordings and personal information remain under your control and protected by your device's security measures.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your recordings (stored in your device's storage)
- Delete your recordings at any time
- Uninstall the App to remove all associated data
